Questions

What counts as a "feedback item"?

Any single inbound piece of customer signal — a support ticket, a sales call transcript, a review, or a survey response — counts as one item toward your monthly volume.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrades apply immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer nonprofit or startup discounts?

Early-stage startups under 20 employees can request a discounted Starter rate — reach out from the contact page.

Have a larger team or custom needs?

Talk to our sales team about Enterprise pricing.
